Jenenson et al 2010
	Hipp-limited lesions
	12 images in sequence, sequence repeated 2x
	Exemplar foil (FC-C), Y/N + confidence judgments (unreported)
	d' for FC-C data: z(H) - z(FA)
		if hit == 1 -> 1- 1/(2*n_targets)
		if fa == 0 -> 1/(2*n_foils)
	d_a for y/n judgements with slope parameter s from ROC
		sqrt(2/(1=s^2)) * (z(hit) - sz(FA))
	s is ratio of std dev of foil distribution to std dev of target distribution (slope)
	4 tests for images of objects; 4 tests for silhouettes

Bayley et al. 2008
